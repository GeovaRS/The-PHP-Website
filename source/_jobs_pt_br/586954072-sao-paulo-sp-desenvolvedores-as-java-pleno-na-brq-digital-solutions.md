# [São Paulo-SP] Desenvolvedores (as) Java Pleno na BRQ Digital Solutions

Frontend: HTML5, JQuery, JS, Bootstrap e Primefaces.

Frameworks: JPA, Hibernate, Spring, e Laravel.
